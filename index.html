Here are some of my projects:

  • 3D Scanned Potato Chips - Product Visualization

    Poly Cam, After Effects, Nuke, Maya and Unreal Engine

    Produced a short promotional video for Don Potato Chips, using a 3D scanner to capture a high-resolution model of the product. Created realistic textures and animated the chips in a stylized sequence to enhance visual appeal and deliver an engaging, high-quality product visualization for marketing use.

  • Realtime Motion Designer: Cloner and Effector

    Unreal Engine

    Motion design project in Unreal Engine, using the Cloner and Effector tools in Motion Designer to create smooth, eye-catching visuals. This project shows what Unreal Engine can do for real-time animation.

  • "Flutter" Butterfly Scene

    Unreal Engine, Maya, Nuke

    During my internship at Academy Gold, I worked as a VFX artist on a short film where our team integrated a CG butterfly into live-action footage. Over the course of 8 days, we completed 30 VFX shots, focusing on realistic lighting, motion tracking, and seamless compositing to bring the butterfly to life within the scene.

    Mentors: Jerome Chen, Jamie Dixon

  • Beyond Earth

    Unreal Engine, Maya, Nuke, Syntheyes

    This project allowed me to explore combining real-time 3D creation with post-production techniques to create a visually immersive scene.

  • Dino Beast Scene

    Maya, Unreal Engine and Nuke

    I worked with my friend Wills Groth on his monster animation. I built the entire environment, rendered the scenes, and did the compositing. It was a fun collaboration that allowed us to explore our creative ideas.

  • Japanese Garden

    Unreal Engine, NukeX

    I created a cinematic Japanese-inspired scene in Unreal Engine, using assets from Megascans to bring a rich, detailed environment to life. This project was all about capturing the beauty and depth of traditional Japanese landscapes, with careful lighting and cinematic angles to evoke a truly immersive feel. The result showcases how Unreal Engine can transform 3D assets into compelling visual storytelling.

  • Spider Flesh Monster

    Unreal Engine, Maya, NukeX

    I added a CG spider monster into live-action footage. I used Maya to place and light the creature, rendered it in Unreal Engine, and finished the shot in Nuke.

  • Aiya Matcha - Product Visualization

    After Effects, Nuke

    Created a short promotional video blending visual effects with live-action footage to enhance product visualization for Aiya Matcha. Designed dynamic visual elements to highlight product features, flavor, and brand identity—delivering an engaging, high-quality asset for marketing use.

  • 29th Store Anniversary

    After Effects, Nuke and Unreal Engine

    Integrated CG elements into live-action footage to enhance a store’s 29th anniversary celebration video. Created and animated a realistic balloon floating behind the storefront, subtly reinforcing the celebratory theme while maintaining a natural look within the scene.

  • XQS - Product Visualization

    After Effects, Maya and Unreal Engine

    Created a short 3D animated promotional video optimized for social media platforms, showcasing product features through dynamic visuals and clean motion design. Used 3D software to model, animate, and render the product, delivering eye-catching content tailored for brand engagement and online visibility.

  • Realtime Previsualization

    Cyclops AR

    I had the opportunity to attend a Real-Time Previs Workshop with Third Floor Company and Dane Allen Smith, where I used their Cyclops AR app to create and film a previsualization. This hands-on experience with advanced AR tools allowed me to explore real-time scene planning and storytelling techniques.
